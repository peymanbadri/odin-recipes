Edible-cookie

Ingredients
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon milk
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
Steps
  1. To heat-treat your flour so it is safe to use:place flour in a microwave-safe dish and cook for 1 munute and 15 seconds, stirring it every 15 seconds.set aside.
  2. Beat sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a large until creamy.Beat in vanilla extract and salt. Add heat-treated flour;mix until a crumbly dough forms.stir in milk until dough is just combined;fold in milk chocolate chips.
